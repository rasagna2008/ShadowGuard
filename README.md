## 🚀 What Is ShadowGuard?

Most security apps ask *"do you know the password?"*

ShadowGuard asks **"are you really YOU?"**

It continuously analyzes your **behavioral biometrics** — the unique way you type, your rhythm, your timing — and generates a live **Identity Confidence Score**. If the score drops because someone else is at your keyboard, it **automatically locks the session** and activates a privacy shield.

To unlock it? You need the right passphrase — typed *the way only you type it*.

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| 🧠 Behavioral Biometrics | Analyzes your unique keystroke timing and rhythm in real time |
